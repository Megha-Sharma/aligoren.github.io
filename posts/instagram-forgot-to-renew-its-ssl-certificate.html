2015-04-30 16:28

Instagram’s website is showing a error right now for many users that the company’s security certificate is invalid.

Error Screenshots

Also you can see error on chrome.

Instagram’s mobile apps seem to be working without issues so the problem just affects the company’s web interface. Evidently even the biggest companies forget to renew their certificates occasionally.
